Furthermore, the gravitational field is governed by a modified Einstein equation where the source term includes the Stress-Energy Tensor of Entanglement (T_{\text{ENT}}).

 * Energy-Dependent Photon Velocity (v_g \neq c): Photons are predicted to slow down in regions of rapid entanglement change.
 * GRB Timing Delay: High-energy photons from distant sources, such as Gamma-Ray Bursts (GRBs), should arrive later than their low-energy counterparts .
The \tau-G-Gravity framework offers a comprehensive, testable path towards geometric unification, challenging the established axiomatic foundations of both quantum mechanics and gravity.
